resetGame() - Generates the initial game objects. You can test this by selecting the 'Reset Game' button

genGrid() - Generates the table. You can test this just by loading the webpage

movePiece() - Moves a given coordinate to another given coordinate

isKing() - checks to see if piece should be king

legalMove() - validates coordinates provided are legal for given piece (this might not be needed since movePiece() might already do this.. including just in case).
